Question 1
What is the order of sharps?
Answer:
F, C, G, D, A, E, B

Question 2
What is the order of flats?
Answer:
B, E, A, D, G, C, F

Question 3
What is the major scale with 3 sharps?
Answer:
A major

Question 4
Which way on the key signature line do you add flats?
Answer:
Left

Question 5
Are the minor keys above or below the line?
Answer:
Below
